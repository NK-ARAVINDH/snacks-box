VEG CUTLET

INGRIDIENTS

  • 2 cups of potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup of French beans, finely chopped
  • 3/4 to 1 cup of carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup of green peas
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Breadcrumbs or flattened rice (poha), for binding and coating
  • Oil, for frying
  • Optional: Cauliflower, beets, spinach, broccoli, sweet corn, soya granules

PROCEDUE:

  1. Steam the potatoes, carrots, beans, and peas until they are soft.
  2. Mash the steamed vegetables together in a large bowl.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, and mix well.
  4. Shape the mixture into patties.
  5. Coat the patties with breadcrumbs or flattened rice.
  6. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  7. Fry the cutlets until they are golden brown on both sides.

TIPS

Squeeze out any excess water from the veggies using a clean cloth or paper towel. This step ensures that your cutlets hold their shape and turn out crispy.
