velvet elixer!

Savor the Velvet Elixir, a sumptuous smoothie that marries the creaminess of coconut milk with the lush flavors of berries and cherries, crowned with a hint of vanilla. üçπ

INGRIDIENTS:

  • 1/2 cup of chilled coconut milk (for a creamy base)
  • 1/4 cup of Greek yogurt (for added richness and protein)
  • 1/2 ripe avocado (for a smooth texture)
  • 1/4 cup of fresh raspberries (for a tart kick)
  • 1 tablespoon of raw honey or agave nectar (for natural sweetness)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (for an aromatic touch)
  • A pinch of ground cinnamon (for warmth)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract (for an aromatic touch)
  • A pinch of ground cinnamon (for warmth)
  • 1 tablespoon of chia seeds (for omega-3s and fiber)
  • 2-3 ice cubes (for a frosty chill)

PROCEDURE:

1. Start by adding the chilled coconut milk and Greek yogurt into your blender. 2.Scoop in the avocado and add the fresh raspberries and pitted cherries. 3.Drizzle in the honey or agave nectar, and add the vanilla extract. 4.Sprinkle in the ground cinnamon and chia seeds for a nutrient boost. 5.Add the ice cubes to give your smoothie a refreshing edge. 6.Blend on high until the mixture is smooth and velvety. 7.Pour into a tall, chilled glass and garnish with a few whole raspberries and a sprig of mint.

TIPS:

Freeze your fruits ahead of time for an extra frosty and thick Velvet Elixir Smoothie without diluting the flavor with ice. üçìü••
