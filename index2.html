MUSHROOM SANDWITCH

Experience the delight of a **Grilled Mushroom Sandwich**, where earthy mushrooms meet aromatic herbs between slices of toasted whole wheat bread. It's a simple yet satisfying meal that's perfect for any time of the day. Enjoy the fusion of flavors in every bite! ðŸ˜Š

INGRIDIENTS

  • 8 slices of Whole Wheat Brown Bread
  • 400 grams of Button Mushrooms, chopped
  • 2 Green Chillies, finely chopped
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • Coriander Leaves, small bunch, finely chopped
  • Butter, salted, as required
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil, for cooking

PROCEDURE:

  1. Savory mushrooms and melty cheese tucked between toasted bread slices create the ultimate comfort food.
  2. Clean and chop mushrooms.
  3. SautÃ© mushrooms with garlic and onions until golden.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  5. Butter bread slices on one side.
  6. Spread mushroom mix on unbuttered side.
  7. Add cheese slices over mushrooms.
  8. Top with another bread slice, buttered side up.
  9. Grill on pan until crispy and cheese melts.
  10. Slice sandwich diagonally.
  11. SERVE HOT WITCH KETCHUP OR MAYONEISE

    12. TIPS:

    CHOOSE THE RIGHT MUSHROOOMS: oyster mushrooms are ideal for their meaty texture and ability and ability to crisp up nicely
